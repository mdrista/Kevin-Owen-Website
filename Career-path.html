Kevin Owen Career Path

Career Path Overview

2020: Owen was found abandoned at a trainstation in Gore, New Zealand.
2021: Joined local football club, quickly became a standout player.
2022: Gets asked to play for Waitaki Boys High School
2023: Joins Waitaki Boys at just 10 years old.
2024: Barcelona send scouts to watch him play.
2025: Invited to join La Masia, Barcelona's youth academy.
Who knows what the future holds for this young talent?
